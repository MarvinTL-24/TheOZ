The Orphanage Years

Dorothy Ashborne spent her early years in a small orphanage in early-1910s Kansas, USA. Her life was marked by loneliness and a deep sense of abandonment.

Introverted and deeply lonely, Dorothy struggled to connect with others. She was prone to intense emotional outbursts when her feelings finally erupted, moments of raw emotion that both frightened and isolated her further.

"Her only companions were memories she couldn't quite grasp, and a sense of loss she couldn't explain."

Before discovering her powers, Dorothy was a vulnerable child struggling with fragmented memories and unexplained trauma. Her world was small, confined to the orphanage walls and the mysteries of her own mind.
