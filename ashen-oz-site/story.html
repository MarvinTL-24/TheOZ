From Orphanage to Ashen World

The Ashen OZ begins not with magic, but with loss. Dorothy's journey starts in the Kansas orphanage where she grew up, a place haunted by half-remembered dreams and whispered secrets.

When the hurricane strikes and transports her to the ashen world, she must quickly adapt to survive her first encounters with Oz's corrupted inhabitants. Each battle is not just a test of skill, but a piece of her forgotten past.
