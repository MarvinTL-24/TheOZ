A) OVERVIEW

The Girl from Kansas

The Ashen OZ tells the story of a young girl whose journey begins in an orphanage in early-1900s Kansas, USA. It follows the inner collapse and search for salvation of Dorothy Ashborne, and presents a grimdark fantasy narrative built around a repeating loop.
