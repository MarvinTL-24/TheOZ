Witch's Umbrella

The Witch's Umbrella is Dorothy's primary weapon, discovered early in her journey through Ashen OZ. This mystical artifact serves as both a defensive tool and an offensive instrument.

"A three-hit basic combo that forms the foundation of all combat gameplay."

The umbrella's combat style emphasizes precision and rhythm. Each of the three hits in the basic combo has different properties:

Ashen Dice - Phantom Face

One of six mystical faces

System Overview

Structured similarly to Hades' 'Boons of the Gods', the Ashen Dice system allows players to roll for powerful upgrades after combat rewards, memory fractures, or specific events.

Mechanics:

  • ‚Ä¢ Choose one of three faces after rolling
  • ‚Ä¢ Assign to skill slots (jump, attack, chained action, etc.)
  • ‚Ä¢ Each face has unique attributes and visual themes
