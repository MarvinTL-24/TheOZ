a. Overview

The Chained System is the core action mechanic that runs through both combat and exploration in The Ashen OZ.

Using "Chains," Dorothy can impale enemies to initiate combat, or embed chains into Dreamcatchers (ÔºùAnchors) to propel herself through space.

By literally stitching together fragments of a broken world, the system organically fuses combat and traversal into a single flow.

b. System Structure

Category Name Role
Chained Attack Combat Drive a chain into an enemy to dash forward and initiate combat. The ignition point of the combat loop
Chained Move Exploration Embed chains into anchors to move, re-jump, or reposition. The driving force of map traversal

Both actions share a single cooldown (5 seconds),
allowing players to experience the speed and fluidity of seamlessly blending combat and movement.

To address a common issue in Metroidvania games‚Äîthe separation and disruption between combat and exploration‚Äî the Chained System in The Ashen OZ is built upon the following design principles:
