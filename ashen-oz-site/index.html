Story

A) Overview

The Ashen OZ tells the story of a young girl whose journey begins in an orphanage in early-1900s Kansas, USA.

It follows the inner collapse and search for salvation of Dorothy Ashborne, and presents a grimdark fantasy narrative built around a repeating loop.

The "Oz" of this story is no longer a land of magic. It is an ashen world, shaped and manifested from Dorothy's wounds, despair, and fragmented memories.

By chance, she comes into possession of a witch's umbrella and a pair of enchanted shoes, imbued with eerie and formidable power.

Through her journey, and through her encounters with the Scarecrow, the Tin Girl, the Lion, and Toto, she begins to piece together her forgotten memories. And at the end of her journey, every story the girl once believed in becomes entangled—revealing an unexpected and shocking truth.

B) Premise

On a stormy night, Dorothy discovers a strange, ashen die hidden away in the attic of her orphanage.

In that very moment, a hurricane engulfs the building, and Dorothy is transported into Ashen OZ.

Within Oz, she embarks on a grotesque yet mysterious journey, seeking to uncover her lost memories—and the secrets buried within them.
