a. Overview

The Chained System is the core action mechanic that runs through both combat and exploration in The Ashen OZ. Using "Chains," Dorothy can impale enemies to initiate combat, or embed chains into Dreamcatchers (＝Anchors) to propel herself through space.

Bestiary

The creatures and enemies that inhabit the ashen world. Learn their weaknesses and strategies.

"All humans in Oz… all living beings—their bodies and minds are diseased, withering away."

Monster Type Classification

No. Type Primary Role Combat Style / AI Behavior
1️⃣ Berserker Frontline Pressure Charges straight at the player; collides with walls to self-stun. Breaks through attacks with super armor.
2️⃣ Soldie Mid-range Melee Short-range slashes, 2–3 hit combos, fast attack tempo.
3️⃣ Scouter Ranged Harassment Maintains distance, fires projectiles (e.g. fireballs), repeats retreat loops.
4️⃣ Invoker Control / Summoning Remains stationary; deploys magic circles, summons minions, or inflicts status effects.
5️⃣ Trapper Area Control Immobile. Continuously emits lasers, flames, or poison. Can throw and place bombs.
6️⃣ Exploder Self-Destruct / Chase Pursues the player and detonates at close range, often inflicting fear effects.
7️⃣ Guardian Defense / Tank Multiple super armor states, shield counters, slow but heavy strikes.
8️⃣ Mid / Final Boss Complex Patterns Multi-phase encounters with chained attacks and cinematic elements.
Sub Bug Aerial / Chain Mobility Primarily used for chained movement and gauge gain; fragile, usually defeated in one hit.
Sub Hybrid Melee ↔ Ranged Switch Alternates between close and long-range attacks to increase mid-late game pattern variety.
