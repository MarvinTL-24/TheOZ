GAME OVERVIEW

What is The Ashen OZ?

The Ashen OZ is a third-person action RPG set in a post-apocalyptic version of the magical land of Oz. What was once a vibrant, colorful paradise is now a desolate wasteland, haunted by twisted versions of familiar characters and creatures.

Play as a survivor navigating this grimdark reimagining, where every memory is tainted by trauma, and every familiar face hides a dark secret. Uncover the truth behind the fall of Oz while battling corrupted entities in fast-paced, souls-like combat.
